EVENT PHOTOGRAPHY

Love Aziz Photography is owned and operated by Latoya Parra, an event photographer based in Southern California. Her approach is simple: When looking back on your album, you need to be able to relive each moment in the most authentic way possible. You deserve it. You can expect a confident, calm and organized photographic experience while capturing the beautiful story of love and life, as it is, to be remembered and cherished forever!
